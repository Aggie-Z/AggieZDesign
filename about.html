A Little About Myself

Hello there! My name is Aggie, and I’m a graphic designer based in Niagara, Ontario. I’m just your average artsy gal who is fueled by iced coffee and my love for books and cats.

As a small girl, I used to draw constantly... on everything... including my bedroom walls much to my dad’s dismay. I could not be stopped! Creating something with my hands and my mind have always brought me joy, which is why I love being a graphic designer so much! There’s no limit to what I can do in terms of creating! I hope that my work can in turn inspire you and create a little joy in your life.

-Aggie
